Opt-In Method

Opt-in to SMS/text messages occurs via webform at https://squiretown.co/contact. This is a web-based opt-in only — it is not a keyword-based or text-to-join opt-in. Users do not text a keyword to a short code or phone number to enroll.

To opt in, a user must affirmatively check a dedicated SMS consent checkbox on the contact form. The checkbox is unchecked by default. No pre-checked boxes are ever used — the user must take a deliberate affirmative action to consent.

The SMS consent checkbox is visually and functionally separate from any Privacy Policy or Terms of Service acceptance. A user can submit the contact form without opting in to SMS messages.

Exact Disclosure Text Shown to Users

The following verbatim text appears in the label next to the SMS consent checkbox on the live form at squiretown.co/contact:

I agree to receive SMS/text messages from Squiretown Consulting, LLC. By checking this box, I consent to receive text messages at the phone number provided above. Message types may include appointment confirmations, appointment reminders, and follow-up messages related to my inquiry. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help or STOP to unsubscribe at any time. Consent is not a condition of purchase or service. See our SMS Terms and Privacy Policy for details.

Consent Audit Trail

Each opt-in is recorded in our database with a timestamp and the exact disclosure text the user agreed to. Consent records are retained for audit purposes and can be produced upon request from a carrier or regulator.
